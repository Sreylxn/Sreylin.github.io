    Who am I? 👩🏻‍💼

    I am a British-Cambodian University of Kent graduate and my friends would describe me as ambitious, hardworking and motivated. I've always believed that no matter how challenging something feels at first, if I break it down into small, manageable steps makes it possible to overcome. That mindset is what's brought me this far and has helped me grown every step of the way.

    Starting out in college studying Graphic Design, never have I imagined the confidence to pursue a STEM degree. But this journey is proof of how far I've come and how much I've grown by pushing through doubt and embracing the unknown.

    Winning an innovation award recognised by CAPACITAS for a collaborative group project that stood out among all final-year Computer Science and Master’s students, kickstarting Cyber Women within the Cyber Security Society, and receiving a Black Hat Europe Scholarship, these are some of the most meaningful highlights of my university experience. They’re achievements I never imagined possible when I first started.

    What's next? 🤷🏻‍♀️

    Finding ways to contribute, grow and keep learning. I'm always looking for ways to make a positive impact, even through small things. I'm passionate in designing and problem solving. Please do take a look at my projects as I'd love to share what I've been working on.
